Past Experience Prompts

This retrospective questionnaire is not meant to be comprehensive,
but to give ideas of some experiences to include and how to describe them.

Hypothetical Typical States:

These are a few prompts to get you to consider the average, unnotable experiences of your daily life: General, average states.
You may answer or ignore any questions, or record many vectors for the same kind of experience.

Example Prompts:
1. average boredom
2. average relaxation
3. average daydreamy state
4. average stress
5. average flow experience
6. average sleepy/drowsy
7. average anger
8. average rage

Extreme/Rare Experiences:

These are a few prompts to get you to consider notable or extreme experiences from your life.
You may answer or ignore any questions, or record many vectors for the same kind of experience
(i.e. Several intense flow experiences, several intense fear experiences, etc.)

Example Prompts:
1. Most intense session/s of meditation/prayer
2. Most intense fear
3. Most intense trauma
4. Most intense sadness
5. Most intense flow
6. Most dissociated
7. Most depressed
8. Most pleasure/ bliss/ joy
9. Major spiritual experiences
10. Major life events
11. Greatest events/feelings of success or accomplishment in your life

Psychoactive Substances (Drugs)

Please list any psychoactive drugs you can recall experiencing, including approximate dosage,
especially those experienced without any other substances, and record a vector using the template for each.
If it was an extreme experience with the substance, please indicate it in the keywords section (i.e. most intense, most extreme, etc.)

Example Substances:
1. Alcohol
2. Cannabis
3. Caffeine
4. Nicotine
5. LSD/Acid
6. Cocaine
7. Heroin
8. Psilocybin/"Magic" Mushrooms
9. Amphetamine
10. DMT
11. Ayahuasca
12. MDMA/Ecstasy
